Sizzlin Sahara

STEAKHOUSE

The steakhouse has been in the city for as long as anyone can remember, and it has become the go-to steakhouse among the locals

The steakhouse is run by a local family and the recipes have been passed down for 5 generations now. The recipes are a reflection of the authentic African taste
